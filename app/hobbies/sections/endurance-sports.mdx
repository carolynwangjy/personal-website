i've been involved with distance running for almost seven years now, starting in high school cross country and track
(where i was varsity captain of cross country for two years and track and field's distance captain for one).
i also raced on uc berkeley's club triathlon team (cal tri) for two years!

nowadays, i primarily run for fun, and am currently dabbling a little in strength training.
here are some of the races i've done over the years and my favorite routes:
