i'm currently taking vocal lessons with the wonderful [thomas truhitte](http://www.thomastruhitte.com/)! my favorite pieces are musical theatre
classics (les mis, chicago, phantom etc.) and broadway pieces for soprano ingénues. i also help organize [heartstrings](https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/~heartstrings/), uc berkeley's music wellness club. if you like casual jam sessions, music performance, or community service, check us out :)

in the past, i did [flute](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLvUe5H_qmeidvCzgC6qQvUjjIj69AB4CQ&si=x2FPQU0wqvPphe6a) for six years (four years in symphony orchestra, two in band) under dr. ming li, piano for eight years, and classical voice for three years.
i still put those hats on every now and then :)

i am thankful for dr. ming li, who taught me the flute and nurtured me throughout middle and high school with his unending enthusiasm.
and i am forever grateful to larisa meller, who taught me all i know about music, watched me grow up with it, and is the reason why music is such an important part of my life in the first place.
